Siberian Tiger

The Siberian Tiger is a population of the tiger subsspecies Panthera tigris native to the Russian Far East etc.

  • Felidae: Fideali
  • Size: 160 - 200 cm
  • Mass: 300 - 384 kg
  • Gender: Male
  • S. Name: Panthera Tigris Altaica

The tiger is reddish-rusty, or rusty-yellow in color, with narrow black transverse stripes.

Bengal Tiger

The Bengal Tiger is a population of the Panthera tigris tigris subsspecies. It ranks among the biggest wild cats alive today.

  • Felidae: Fideali
  • Height: 90 - 110 cm
  • Mass: 200 - 260 kg
  • Gender: Male
  • S. Name: Panthera Tigris Tigris

The tiger's coat is yellow to light orange, with strips ranging form dark brown to black.
